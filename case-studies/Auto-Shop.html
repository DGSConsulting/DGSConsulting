From Chaos to Clarity

How we helped an auto shop streamline operations and boost productivity

The Challenge

This auto shop was struggling with multiple disconnected systems and inefficient workflows. Techs were frustrated, bays sat empty, and jobs piled up.

The Solution

We integrated Shopmonkey with QuickBooks, streamlined digital workflows, and automated key reporting. This gave the team full visibility into their operations in real time.

The Results

Within weeks, the shop experienced a measurable turnaround:

  • Reduced idle bay time by 30%
  • Shortened job turnaround by 25%
  • Improved visibility and communication between techs and front office

Client Feedback
