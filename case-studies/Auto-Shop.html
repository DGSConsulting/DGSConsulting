How One Auto Shop Recovered 120 Hours Per Month (Without Hiring Anyone)

A 6-month transformation that turned hidden inefficiencies into $23,400 in annual savings

Share on LinkedIn Email

The Challenge

The auto shop was drowning in administrative work and duplicative processes. Technicians and admin staff were constantly frustrated by disorganized systems, manual data entry, and disconnected software.

Key Pain Points

  • Shop management software not integrated with QuickBooks
  • Manual bookkeeping for invoices and payments
  • Technicians spending hours on admin tasks instead of repairs

The Approach

DGS Consulting conducted an operational audit and implemented process documentation, integration of QuickBooks with Shopmonkey, and automated routine administrative workflows.

Implemented Solutions

  • Automated data sync between Shopmonkey and QuickBooks
  • Created SOPs for repetitive administrative tasks
  • Trained staff on new systems to reduce friction
