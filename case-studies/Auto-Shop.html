Automotive

Client Type: Mid-sized independent auto repair shop

Challenge

Disjointed systems and manual tracking meant the owner was losing visibility into job progress and payroll accuracy. Despite strong sales, profit margins were shrinking — and no one could pinpoint why.

Approach

DGS Consulting audited their workflow across scheduling, invoicing, and payroll systems. By identifying redundant data entry and non-billable admin time, we built a simplified system map and introduced automation where it mattered most.

Results

2–3 hrs saved weekly per tech $10–20K annual recovery $1–2K monthly cost reduction
“I thought I needed new software — turns out I just needed a better process. Darren made it simple, and the numbers speak for themselves.”

Key Takeaway

You don’t always need new tools to solve operational problems. Streamlining existing systems can unlock hidden profit and free your team to focus on what matters — the work itself.
