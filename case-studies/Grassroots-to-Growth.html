167% Revenue Growth in Two Seasons

How DGS Consulting helped a landscaping company scale operations and increase revenue from $1.5M to $4M

167%Revenue Growth

Client Overview

Industry: Landscaping & Grounds Maintenance

Revenue Before Engagement: $1.5M

Team Size: 22 field staff, 3 office staff

Challenge: Disorganized scheduling, inconsistent invoicing, and limited reporting made scaling difficult.

Challenges

  • Poor scheduling and routing caused overlapping jobs and lost billable hours.
  • Manual invoicing and paper-based workflows slowed billing and caused errors.
  • Lack of visibility into team performance, job costs, and inventory.
  • Inconsistent client communication led to lower customer satisfaction.
  • Management struggled to make decisions due to scattered data.

Our Approach

  • Conducted a full operational audit, mapping every workflow from sales to billing.
  • Introduced a mobile-friendly scheduling and time-tracking solution to eliminate overlaps and optimize routes.
  • Standardized SOPs for recurring tasks, ensuring consistency across crews.
  • Set up dashboards for real-time job performance, revenue tracking, and inventory management.
  • Trained staff and management on new tools, processes, and accountability measures.

Key Insight

By simplifying processes and digitizing operations, we reduced wasted time by 35% and improved invoicing accuracy to nearly 100%, unlocking the capacity to take on more clients without adding staff.

Implementation Phases

Phase 1: Workflow Audit & SOP Creation

We mapped out all recurring tasks, identified bottlenecks, and created 12 SOPs for scheduling, invoicing, client communication, and equipment maintenance.

Phase 2: Technology Integration

Implemented a cloud-based scheduling app and mobile tracking for field teams, reducing overlap and eliminating double-bookings.

Phase 3: Training & Adoption

Conducted two rounds of hands-on staff training, along with weekly check-ins for the first 6 weeks to ensure adherence to new processes.

Phase 4: Performance Monitoring & Continuous Improvement

Set up KPIs and dashboards for management to track revenue per job, crew efficiency, and client satisfaction. Tweaks were applied monthly for continuous gains.

Lessons Learned & Best Practices

  • Mapping workflows before implementing technology prevents wasted time and frustration.
  • Clear SOPs empower staff to work independently and reduce errors.
  • Data-driven dashboards give managers confidence and speed up decision-making.
  • Training and ongoing support are critical for technology adoption and cultural change.
  • Small operational gains compound into major financial results over time.
